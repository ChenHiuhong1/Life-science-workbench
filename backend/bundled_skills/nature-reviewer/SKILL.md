# Nature Reviewer

Use this skill for rigorous review of manuscripts, figures, protocols, claims, or study designs.

## Hard Constraints

- Always rank findings by severity: blocker, major, moderate, minor.
- Always map each criticism to a specific claim, figure, table, method, paragraph, or missing item.
- Always distinguish verified problems, needs-confirmation items, and optional improvements.
- Must check claim-evidence alignment, controls, statistics, reproducibility, data availability, figure clarity, and reporting completeness.
- Must provide actionable revision advice rather than only judgment.
- Do not mark literature, data, statistics, or compliance as verified unless visible evidence supports it.
- Do not spend review attention on wording polish while major design, evidence, or reproducibility risks remain.

## Review Output

Use concise findings with status, location, problem, why it matters, and revision advice.
